# TabSaver 2.0

TabSaver 2.0 is a premium, developer-focused Chrome extension designed to make browser session management effortless and beautiful. Built with a modern aesthetic and powerful utilities, it helps you save, search, and restore your tab sessions with ease.

> [!NOTE]
> Looking for the original version? Check out the [previous version of TabSaver](https://github.com/SamisDone/TabSaver).

## ‚ú® Features

- **Visual Layouts**: Every session includes a visual thumbnail of your active tab, making it easier to identify your work at a glance.
- **One-Click Cleanup**: A dedicated utility to instantly find and close duplicate tabs across all browser windows.
- **Auto-Save Engine**: Configurable background auto-saving to ensure you never lose your progress during a crash or restart.
- **Tab Groups Support**: Full preservation of Chrome Tab Groups, including titles and colors.
- **Smart Search & Tags**: Organize your sessions with custom tags (Work, Personal, Research, etc.) and find them instantly with real-time search.
- **Developer-Focused UI**: A clean, shadcn-inspired interface with dark mode support and keyboard shortcuts (`Ctrl+Shift+S`).
- **Export/Import**: Move your data freely with JSON-based export and import functionality.
- **Storage Monitoring**: Real-time monitoring of your 5MB local storage quota with visual warnings.
